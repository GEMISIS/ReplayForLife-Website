Replay for Life

CNU's ACM team is proud to be supporting extra life, an organization
that has committed to helping those with acute lymphoblastic leukemia.
We as the gaming community have decided to help them as they help others.




This fall semester, the ACM will be hosting it's replay for life. This is the second Replay for Life event! Come on out to support our charity event. This year, we will be donating the money to a different organization.
